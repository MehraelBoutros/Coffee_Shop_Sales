## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales transactions from a coffee shop to uncover patterns in customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and operational performance. The goal is to identify key insights that can help improve business decisions such as staffing, inventory planning, and product offerings.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains detailed transaction records from a coffee shop, including product information, transaction timestamps, and revenue values.

### Main Sheet
**Transactions**
- Contains 149,000+ sales records
- Includes detailed information about each purchase

### Key Columns
- transaction_id – Unique identifier for each transaction
- transaction_date – Date when the purchase occurred
- transaction_time – Time of the transaction
- store_location – Coffee shop branch location
- product_category – Category of the product
- product_type – Type of product sold
- product_detail – Specific product name
- Revenue – Revenue generated from the transaction

### Other Sheets
- **KPIs** – Key performance indicators such as total revenue
- **Pivots** – Pivot tables summarizing revenue and transaction counts
- **Insights** – Key findings and recommendations
- **Dashboards** – Visualization space for the Excel dashboard

## Project Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends over time
- Identify peak sales hours and days
- Determine top-performing products
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Support business decision-making with data insights

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- KPI Analysis

## Key Insights
- Revenue tends to increase toward the middle of the year
- Most transactions occur during morning hours
- Certain product categories generate significantly higher revenue

## Potential Business Recommendations
- Increase staffing during peak morning hours
- Promote best-selling products
- Improve inventory planning during high-demand periods
